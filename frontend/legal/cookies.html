Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Finance Management System (Finance) uses cookies and related technologies to improve security, performance, and user experience.

1. Purpose of Cookies

Cookies help authenticate users, manage sessions, remember preferences, and ensure system security.

2. Security & Authentication

Secure cookies verify login sessions and prevent unauthorized access. All authentication tokens are encrypted.

3. Performance Monitoring

We use analytical cookies to monitor page load times, optimize rendering performance, and improve application speed.

4. Personalization

Cookies store selected language preferences (EN/HI), dashboard layout choices, and notification settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Third-party integrations may use limited cookies for analytics. No personal data is sold or shared.

6. User Control

You may disable cookies in browser settings. However, login and secure features may not function properly.

7. Updates & Compliance

This policy complies with modern data protection standards and may be updated when necessary.
